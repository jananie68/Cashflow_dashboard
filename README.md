Cash Flow Forecasting Dashboard – Description

The Cash Flow Forecasting Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to monitor, analyze, and forecast an organization’s cash inflows and outflows. It provides real-time financial visibility and helps management make data-driven decisions to maintain healthy liquidity and financial stability.

🔹 Key Objectives

Track total cash inflow and outflow

Monitor net cash flow trends

Forecast future cash positions

Identify potential cash shortages or surplus

Support strategic financial planning

🔹 Key Features

📈 Cash Inflow & Outflow Analysis – Visual representation of income and expenses over time.

📊 Net Cash Flow Calculation – Automatically calculates the difference between inflows and outflows.

📅 Monthly/Quarterly Forecasting – Predicts future cash trends using historical data.

🎯 Variance Analysis – Compares actual vs forecasted cash flow.

📌 Interactive Filters & Slicers – Filter by date, department, category, or business unit.

📉 Trend Analysis Charts – Line and bar charts to identify seasonal patterns.

🔹 KPIs Included

Total Cash Inflow

Total Cash Outflow

Net Cash Flow

Opening Balance

Closing Balance

Forecasted Cash Position
